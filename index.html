CATALOX's risk engine aggregates data from multiple live sources: real-time weather feeds (IMD, OpenWeather), port and customs clearance databases, satellite AIS vessel tracking, road congestion feeds (HERE Maps), railway network bulletins from Indian Railways, and historical delay patterns across 200,000+ routes. Risk scores are recalculated every 15 minutes and surfaced as delay multipliers per transport mode.

Freight cost estimates are based on distance (km) × cargo weight (kg) × mode rate × speed tier. Current Indian market base rates: Road ₹15/km/tonne · Rail ₹8/km/tonne · Air ₹120/km/tonne · Sea ₹4/km/tonne. Additional line items include fuel surcharge (~16%), handling & insurance (~13%), and a 2% platform fee. Speed tiers add 0% (Economy), 35% (Standard), or 75% (Express) to the total. All figures are estimates; final invoices are issued by your carrier.

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). CATALOX is SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, and adheres to India's DPDP Act (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023). Shipment data is never shared with third parties or used for model training without explicit consent. Enterprise customers can opt for private cloud or on-premise deployment for complete data sovereignty.
